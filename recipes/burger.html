Burger

Description

The Classic Burger is a timeless favorite made with a juicy grilled beef patty nestled in a toasted bun. It's typically layered with crisp lettuce, ripe tomato, onions, pickles, cheese, and your favorite condiments. Whether grilled or cooked on a stovetop, this burger delivers savory, satisfying flavor in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb ground beef (80/20 preferred)
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 1 slice cheddar cheese (optional)
  • Lettuce, tomato slices, pickles, and onion
  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayo (as desired)

Steps

  1. Form the ground beef into a patty and season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. Cook the patty in a skillet or grill over medium-high heat for 3â€“4 minutes per side.
  3. Add cheese during the last minute of cooking if desired.
  4. Toast the bun lightly in a skillet or toaster.
  5. Assemble the burger: place the patty on the bottom bun and layer with lettuce, tomato, onion, and pickles.
  6. Top with desired condiments and the top bun.
  7. Serve immediately with fries or a side of your choice.
